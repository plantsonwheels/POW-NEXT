# Plants on Wheels

Bringing nature to your doorstep. Order healthy plants and book trusted gardening services across India.

## What You Can Do

- Browse indoor and outdoor plants with photos and key benefits
- Enquire for any plant directly from its product page
- Book professional gardening services for homes, offices, and communities
- Request custom setups, terrace/balcony makeovers, and corporate gifting

## How To Order Plants

- Open the Products section and choose a plant you like
- Click Enquire Now and submit the short form
- Our team contacts you to confirm availability, price, delivery time, and care tips

## Gardening Services

- Explore services on the homepage and submit an enquiry
- Share your location and preferred date
- A coordinator will call to finalize schedule and pricing

## Delivery & Setup

- Available in select cities; availability varies by product and quantity
- Delivery and setup costs are shared during confirmation
- Plants are quality-checked and packaged for safe transport

## Key Pages

**Public Pages:**

- `/` - Landing page with hero, services, featured products
- `/products` - Product catalog with filters
- `/products/[id]` - Individual product details
- `/about` - Company information and values
- `/contact` - Contact form and information
- `/enquiry` - General enquiry form

**Admin Pages:**

- `/admin/products` - Product management
- `/admin/upload` - Add new products
- `/admin/enquiry` - View all enquiries
